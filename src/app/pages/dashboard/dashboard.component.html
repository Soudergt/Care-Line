Your Line to Care, Right Away!

Caretaker

CareLine provides a wide variety of tools that allow each caretaker to easily record their patients health status, medications and any further treatment the patient may need.

 Patient

CareLine provides patients a place to check all of their medical information and records so they can feel comfortable knowing they are getting the best possible care.

Family

CareLine provides families peace of mind by giving each family tools to keep in contact with their family member being cared for as well as a place to view notes and records taken by the caretaker.
